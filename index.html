Overview

Aims and goals

LAIDS investigates, develops, and evaluates lightweight AI-based Intrusion Detection Systems for resource-constrained networks, targeting strong detection and efficient deployment on edge-class hardware.

  • Benchmarks CNN, CNN-LSTM/GRU, DBN, PCA-CNN, AE-MLP, and GAN variants
  • Evaluates quantisation (FP16, INT8 Dynamic, INT8 Full) and footprint
  • Assesses zero-day generalisation at fixed false-positive budgets
